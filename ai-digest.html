Research & Papers

LACE: Lattice Attention for Cross-thread Exploration

ArXiv cs.AI
  • Introduces a framework where parallel LLM reasoning chains communicate via attention rather than running in isolation and failing redundantly.
  • Cross-thread interaction lets trajectories course-correct based on what sibling chains discovered, reducing repeated dead-ends in hard reasoning tasks.
  • Addresses the core inefficiency of sampling-based methods like Best-of-N without requiring a separate verifier model.

Cross-Family Speculative Decoding for Polish LLMs on Apple Silicon

ArXiv cs.CL
  • Demonstrates speculative decoding between models from different tokenizer families using a UAG (Universal Alignment Gadget) extension to MLX-LM on Apple Silicon.
  • Targets Bielik 11B (Polish-language) — shows the technique generalizes beyond English and same-family model pairs.
  • Cross-family speculative decoding on unified memory (Apple M-series) can still yield meaningful speedups even with tokenizer mismatch overhead.

Brain-CLIPLM: Decoding Compressed Semantic Representations in EEG for Language Reconstruction

ArXiv cs.CL
  • Uses CLIP-style contrastive alignment to bridge non-invasive EEG signals and LM embeddings for sentence-level language reconstruction.
  • Addresses the fundamental SNR and bandwidth limits of EEG by working in compressed semantic space rather than raw token prediction.
  • Sentence-level linguistic structure is recoverable from EEG even when word-level timing is too noisy — an important result for non-invasive BCI research.

DeepER-Med: Advancing Deep Evidence-Based Research in Medicine Through Agentic AI

ArXiv cs.AI
