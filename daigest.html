How it works

🧠

On-Device AI

A real LLM runs on your phone. It reads your articles, picks the best ones, and writes insight-driven takeaways — not just summaries.

📡

Any Topic, Any Source

AI, robotics, crypto, gaming — type a topic and dAIgest auto-discovers RSS feeds and communities. Or add your own sources manually.

🔒

Fully Private

No cloud processing, no data collection, no account. Your articles and reading habits never leave your device.

⏰

Scheduled Updates

Set it and forget it. dAIgest fetches new articles on your schedule — every 6 hours, daily, or weekly — even when the app is closed.

🔌

Plug In Any Model

Browse and download GGUF models from HuggingFace, or import your own. Smaller models run faster, larger ones curate better.

📰

Multiple Digests

Create as many digest pages as you want. Each has its own topic, sources, categories, and schedule.

Three steps to your first digest

1

Download a model

Open Settings and grab a small LLM (469 MB). One tap, runs on any modern phone.

2

Create a digest

Name your topic — like "AI News" — and tap Auto-Discover. dAIgest finds the best sources for you.

3

Pull to curate

Swipe down and watch the AI select, analyze, and summarize the day's most important articles.
