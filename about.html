Who We Are

Densanon LLC is a software company based in Post Falls, Idaho. We're a small team that builds software products and provides development services for businesses of all sizes.

Founded in 2026, our mission is straightforward: build quick, working software solutions. We focus on shipping practical tools rather than chasing complexity. Whether it's a website for a local business, an accounting system for a growing organization, or an AI-powered app that runs entirely on your device, we build things that work.
