Built for Real Accounting

Everything you need to run multi-entity finances — from journal entries to consolidated statements.

📚

General Ledger

Hierarchical chart of accounts, journal entries with approval workflows, fiscal period management, and cached account balances.

💰

Accounts Payable

Vendor management, bills with approval workflows, purchase orders with receipt tracking, payments, debit memos, and 1099 reporting.

💳

Accounts Receivable

Customer management, invoices, estimates with conversion, payments, credit memos, finance charges, aging reports, and statements.

🏢

Multi-Entity Management

Parent companies, subsidiaries, branches, and divisions with ownership tracking. Switch entity context instantly.

📈

Financial Reporting

Trial balance, income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statements with filtering, comparisons, and PDF export.

🔒

Audit & Compliance

Append-only audit log with old/new values and IP tracking. Fiscal period locking, approval workflows, and role-based access control.

🏦

Bank Reconciliation

Reconcile bank statements with auto-matching rules. Import transactions and match them against your ledger entries.

📅

Budgeting

Create budgets with approval workflows, track actuals vs. budget, and analyze variances by account and period.

🔄

Recurring Transactions

Automate recurring journal entries, invoices, and bills on configurable schedules. Never miss a recurring charge.
