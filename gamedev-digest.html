Shift Up has acquired Resident Evil creator Shinji Mikami's new studio

Game Developer
  • Stellar Blade developer Shift Up has acquired Unbound, the Tokyo-based studio founded by Shinji Mikami after his departure from Tango Gameworks in 2023.
  • Unbound was working on console and PC titles — Mikami's first independent creative venture since leaving the studio he founded (Tango).
  • This is one of the more significant acquisition stories in recent months, pairing a breakout Korean action developer with one of the most influential game designers in horror and action history.

Rec Room shutting down after failing to become 'sustainably profitable'

Game Developer
  • Rec Room is closing in June 2026 after years of operation, with leadership stating costs consistently overwhelmed revenue — a structural unit-economics failure common in UGC social platforms.
