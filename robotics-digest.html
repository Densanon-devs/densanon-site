Research & Papers

Robot Talk Episode 153 - Origami-inspired robots, with Chenying Liu

Robohub
  • Oxford researcher Chenying Liu discusses morphological computation -- the idea that a robot's physical structure can actively participate in sensing, processing, and movement, not just actuation.
  • Origami-inspired designs allow robots to achieve complex behaviors through passive mechanical compliance rather than requiring more sensors or compute.
  • This approach points toward robots that are simpler, lighter, and more robust by offloading intelligence into physical form.
