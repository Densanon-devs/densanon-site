Run thousands of solo games to measure how consistently your deck executes its game plan. See kill turn distribution, mulligan rates, and objective pass rates.

Version Tracking

Save deck snapshots, compare any two versions, see score deltas and trend analysis. Track your deck's improvement over time.

Castability Analysis

Per-card mana base check. Find demanding cards you can't reliably cast on curve and get specific recommendations to fix them.

Report Export

Export full analysis as JSON, Markdown, or styled HTML reports. Perfect for sharing builds, getting feedback, or archiving.
